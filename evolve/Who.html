Who Are Evolution?

Evolution is the 1996 UK National Science Fiction Convention happening over Easter weekend, 5-8th April 1996, at the Radisson Edwardian Hotel, near London's Heathrow Airport.

Guests of honour are Vernor Vinge, Colin Greenland, Bryan Talbot, and Dr Jack Cohen.

Evolution's (alphabetical) commitee are:

Mary Branscombe
Simon Bisson
Mark Charsley
Steve Glover
Bridget Hardcastle
Pat McMurray
Graham Taylor
